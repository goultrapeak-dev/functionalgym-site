Services

Two disciplines, done to a standard.

Whether it's one machine or a whole floor, the standard is the same. We fix what's broken, and we keep everything else from breaking — one-off call-outs or ongoing service contracts.

01 — Repair

Repair & replacement

On-site diagnosis, a written quote before anything is touched, and a warranty on the finished job.

  • Treadmill motors, belts, decks & consoles
  • Cable machines — cables, pulleys, weight stacks
  • Elliptical, bike & rower drive systems
  • Rack, bench & frame hardware
  • Part sourcing & full unit replacement
02 — Maintenance

Maintenance & cleaning

Scheduled servicing and deep cleaning that extends equipment life and prevents the breakdowns we get called out for. Ideal for facilities that can't afford downtime.

  • Preventive maintenance & service contracts
  • Belt tracking, tensioning & lubrication
  • Full safety inspection — cables, bolts, stops
  • Deep clean & sanitising of contact surfaces
  • Written condition report after every visit
Equipment we service

Cardio, strength and everything between.

Every type of machine and free-weight setup you'll find on a gym floor — kept safe, clean and running.

How it works

Three steps, no surprises.

Tell us what's wrong

Call or send a photo of the machine and its label. Brand and model let us arrive with the right parts already in the van.

Fixed & guaranteed

About us

We're family owned and locally owned. There's no call centre and no rotating cast of subcontractors — the crew at your door is the crew you spoke to on the phone.

Our job is simple: keep your equipment functional, efficient and genuinely safe to train on, whatever the size of your facility.
